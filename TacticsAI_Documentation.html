    Quickstart

    - First, we encourage all sport enthusiasts to try our search engine.

    - With a few keywords, you will be presented with direct, relevent, and insightful game tactics that are hard to retrieve using social media platforms.

    - Next, if your a coach or player inspiring to imrove your game, you can use our game feedback AI to get feedback for how to improve. TacticsAI is trained using tactical data - most notably video and tactical philosophy of world reknown coaches.

    - Also, use the interactive game board for planning and teaching. There will be AI advice to point out the benefits and potential risks of your personal game plan.

    - Finally, be reminded that all of your tactical ideas can be stored in MyTactics. You can come back to these tactics you have created to find continuous inspiration.
